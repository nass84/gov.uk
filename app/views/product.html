UX Improvements - NHS Scotland

In this user research case study, the focus was on enhancing the FNP Navigation Page for accessing operational and new outcome reports. The study involved six users familiar with the FNP website, employing both unmoderated lookback studies and one-on-one Teams interviews to explore user experience, navigation, and satisfaction.

AR VR APP - Innovate Grant

The Pagemaster project combined augmented reality (AR) and virtual reality (VR) with storytelling, creating an interactive app for children, especially those in hospitals. It enabled users to craft stories and bring them to life in a 3D environment, using a GPT-like language model. This fusion of technology and creativity not only entertained but also educated, offering a unique outlet for children’s imagination and supporting their emotional wellbeing during hospital stays.

Young Persons Hospital Journal - Bristol Children’s Hospital

Our mission was to enhance the wellbeing of young people undergoing medical treatment by providing them with a journal platform. This platform will serve as a space for them to reflect on and document their hospital journey. The platform will also allow for easy sharing of these entries with loved ones, promoting a sense of connectedness and support even from a distance.
